# DFT Campaign and Claim Scope

The profile expands substrate/additive × catalyst model × coordination mode × conformer × charge × multiplicity. Pruning must be recorded before production comparisons. A single converged complex cannot establish a general coordination preference.

Claim strength rises from coordination tendency to relative binding, elementary-step mechanism, poisoning hypothesis, established poisoning, and industrial performance. Each level requires additional evidence. Isolated molecular DFT may support a bounded coordination/binding or elementary-step claim; it cannot by itself establish catalyst lifetime, plant poisoning, productivity or process performance.
