# DFT Uncertainty and Sensitivity Budget

A numerical DFT result should carry the dominant model and method sensitivities rather than a false universal error bar. Record conformer/spin/model alternatives, method/basis/dispersion/solvent sensitivity, numerical convergence, low-frequency and standard-state conventions, and reference-state uncertainty.

Use `report_separately` when components are not independent statistical errors. Root-sum-square is acceptable only when independence and interpretation are justified. Rank/qualitative robustness may be accepted even when absolute values are uncertain; state that distinction explicitly.
