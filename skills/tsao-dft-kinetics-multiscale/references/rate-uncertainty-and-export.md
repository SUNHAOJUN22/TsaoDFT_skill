# Rate Uncertainty and External Handoffs

Barrier uncertainty maps exponentially into rate uncertainty. Report the barrier convention, molecularity, standard state, degeneracy, transmission coefficient and temperature with every TST rate. A barrier-only interval omits mechanism, solvent friction, diffusion, coverage and transport uncertainty.

Cantera/CatMAP/Pyomo exports are handoffs until complete thermodynamic models, dimensional rate expressions, phase/site definitions and downstream validation are supplied.
