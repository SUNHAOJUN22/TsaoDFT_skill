# TsaoDFT Repository Guardrails

1. Work directly on `main`; never create feature, repair, release or temporary branches.
2. Preserve raw inputs/outputs and record hashes.
3. Do not invent structures, charge, multiplicity, states, basis/ECP, pseudopotentials, U values, standard states or reaction connectivity.
4. `completed`, `validated` and `accepted` are distinct.
5. Cross-Skill handoffs carry provenance and unresolved assumptions.
6. Specialized profiles never auto-load outside their declared scope.
7. Expensive or destructive execution requires explicit approval.
8. AI diagrams are illustrative only and never quantitative evidence.
9. Upstream licenses and licensed scientific software remain external.
10. Every README/demo figure is labeled synthetic unless it derives from accepted real data.
