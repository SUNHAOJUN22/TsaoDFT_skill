# Reporting and delivery

## Project deliverables

- calculation plan and open-decision list;
- original and generated inputs;
- logs/checkpoints/wavefunction files;
- parser and validation reports;
- energy/property tables with units and references;
- analysis scripts/menu inputs and version record;
- figure specification, source data, VMD scripts and rendered assets;
- claim-evidence matrix and accepted-claim report;
- limitations, contradictions and follow-up tasks.

## Methods writing

Report enough detail to reproduce the observable: software/version,
functional/method, basis/ECP, dispersion, solvent, charge/multiplicity, conformer
scope, optimization/frequency/TS/IRC protocol, thermal/standard-state correction,
TD/NMR/NBO/Multiwfn settings, grid/isovalue and rendering parameters.

## Figure caption minimum

- molecule/state/orbital or analysis identity;
- computational level and solvent when relevant;
- isovalue/surface and color/sign convention;
- energy/potential units and reference;
- whether scales are shared across panels;
- statistical/split details for ML plots.

## Final bundle

Prefer editable SVG/PDF for numerical panels, 600-dpi TIFF or high-resolution PNG
for molecular renders, clean CSV/TSV source data and a QA note. Inspect every
export at final journal size.
