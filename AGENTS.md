# TsaoDFT Repository Guardrails

1. Work directly on `main`; never create feature, repair, release or temporary branches.
2. DFT is the scientific centre: structure, HPC, ML, kinetics and figures must preserve the electronic-structure model and provenance.
3. Preserve raw inputs/outputs and record hashes; never overwrite source evidence silently.
4. Do not invent structures, charge, multiplicity, states, basis/ECP, pseudopotentials, Hubbard U, standard states, reference energies or reaction connectivity.
5. `planned`, `prepared`, `completed`, `validated`, `accepted` and `claim accepted` are distinct.
6. Declare engine support as L0/L1/L2/L3. Never describe a reference/handoff as execution-tested.
7. Cross-Skill handoffs carry artifact hashes, method fingerprints, support levels, unresolved assumptions, cost and approval status.
8. Specialized profiles never auto-load outside their declared scope and may not weaken universal DFT gates.
9. Expensive, remote, destructive or cost-escalating execution requires explicit approval.
10. AI diagrams are illustrative only and never quantitative evidence.
11. Upstream licenses, scientific executables, POTCAR, pseudopotentials and basis/potential libraries remain external.
12. Every README/demo figure is visibly labelled synthetic unless it derives from accepted real data.
